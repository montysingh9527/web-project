Pork (猪肉)：[pɔːrk] They prepared a delicious dish using tender pork. 他们准备了一道美味的菜肴，使用了嫩猪肉。
Chop (猪排)：[tʃɒp] She grilled the pork chop to perfection. 她将猪排烤得恰到好处。
Streaky pork/marbled beef (五花肉 / 有理肉)： [ˈstriːki pɔːrk / ˈmɑːrbəld biːf] The marbling in the beef gives it a rich flavor. 牛肉中的大理石纹理赋予了它丰富的味道。
Fatty meat (肥肉)：[ˈfæti miːt] Some people prefer lean meat over fatty meat. 有些人更喜欢瘦肉而不是肥肉。
Lean meat (瘦肉)：[liːn miːt] She chose lean meat for her diet to reduce fat intake. 她选择了瘦肉作为饮食，以减少脂肪摄入。
Fore leg (前腿)：[fɔːr lɛɡ] The fore leg of the animal is used to make flavorful dishes. 动物的前腿用于制作美味的菜肴。
Hind leg (后腿)：[haɪnd lɛɡ] The hind leg of the animal is often used for roasting. 动物的后腿常用于烤制。
Pettitoes (猪蹄)：[ˌpɛtɪˈtoʊz] The dish is made from stewed pettitoes with spices. 这道菜由炖煮的猪蹄与香料制成。
Hock & Foot (脚爪)：[hɒk & fʊt] The hock and foot of the pig are used to make traditional soups. 猪的脚爪常用于制作传统汤料。
Sinew (蹄筋)：[ˈsɪnjuː] The dish includes tender pieces of sinew for added texture. 这道菜包含嫩的蹄筋块，增添了口感。
Gristle (软骨)：[ˈɡrɪsəl] He removed the gristle from the meat before cooking. 他在烹饪前将肉中的软骨去除。
Beef roast (牛肉烤肉)：[biːf roʊst] They enjoyed a Sunday dinner of succulent beef roast. 他们享受了一顿多汁的周日牛肉烤肉晚餐。
Veal (小牛肉)：[viːl] The delicate flavor of veal is often used in gourmet dishes. 小牛肉的细腻味道常用于美食菜肴中。
Ground beef (碎牛肉)：[ɡraʊnd biːf] She used ground beef to make tasty meatballs. 她用碎牛肉制作美味的肉丸。
Steak (牛排)：[steɪk] He ordered a medium-rare steak at the restaurant. 他在餐厅点了一份五分熟的牛排。
Silverside (牛腿肉)：[ˈsɪlvərsaɪd] Silverside is often used for making deli-style roast beef. 牛腿肉常用于制作熟食店风格的烤牛肉。
Sirloin (牛腰肉)：[ˈsɜːrlɔɪn] The sirloin steak is known for its tenderness and flavor. 牛腰肉牛排以其嫩度和味道而闻名。
Mutton (羊肉)：[ˈmʌtn] The stew was made with tender mutton and flavorful spices. 这道炖菜是用嫩羊肉和美味的香料制作的。
Shrimp (虾)：[ʃrɪmp] She ordered a plate of garlic shrimp for an appetizer. 她点了一份蒜蓉虾作为开胃菜。
Crab (螃蟹)：[kræb] They cracked open the crab shells to enjoy the tender meat inside. 他们打开螃蟹壳，享受里面嫩滑的肉。
Fish (鱼)： [fɪʃ] He caught a big fish while fishing by the lake. 他在湖边钓鱼时钓到了一条大鱼。
```
##### 豆制品
```
Tou-fu / Beancurd (豆腐)：[toʊ fuː / ˈbiːnˌkɜːrd] She used tofu to make a delicious vegan stir-fry. 她用豆腐制作了一道美味的素食炒菜。
Bean sheets (豆腐衣)： [biːn ʃiːts] The dish is wrapped in delicate bean sheets. 这道菜用精致的豆腐衣包裹着。
Bean starch noodles (粉丝)：[biːn stɑːrtʃ ˈnuːdəlz] Bean starch noodles are commonly used in Asian soups. 粉丝经常用于亚洲汤料中。
Bean starch sheets (粉皮)：[biːn stɑːrtʃ ʃiːts] The dish is served with delicate bean starch sheets. 这道菜配有精致的粉皮。
Bean curd strips (豆腐干丝)：[biːn kɜːrd strɪps] Bean curd strips add texture to the salad. 豆腐干丝为沙拉增添了口感。
Pickled beancurd (腐乳)：[ˈpɪkl̩d ˈbiːnˌkɜːrd] She enjoys the unique flavor of pickled beancurd. 她喜欢腐乳独特的风味。
Fried gluten puff (油面筋)：[fraɪd ˈɡluːtən pʌf] The dish includes crispy fried gluten puff. 这道菜包含了脆脆的油面筋块。
Fried beancurd puff (油豆腐)：[fraɪd ˈbiːnˌkɜːrd pʌf] They served fried beancurd puff as an appetizer. 他们将油豆腐块作为开胃菜供应。
Fermented black bean (豆豉)：[ˈfɜːrmɛntɪd blæk biːn] Fermented black beans are used to season many dishes. 豆豉被用于调味许多菜肴。
Fermented soybean (豆酱)：[ˈfɜːrmɛntɪd ˈsɔɪbiːn] Fermented soybean paste adds depth to the flavor of the soup. 发酵的豆酱为汤的味道增添了层次。
Beancurd sheets (百叶)：[ˈbiːnˌkɜːrd ʃiːts] Beancurd sheets are used to wrap various fillings. 百叶被用来包裹各种馅料。
Dried beancurd (豆腐干)：[draɪd ˈbiːnˌkɜːrd] Dried beancurd is often marinated and used in stir-fries. 豆腐干常被腌制后用于炒菜。
